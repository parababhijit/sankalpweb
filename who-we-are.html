Sankalp Micro Association

Sankalp Micro Association, founded in the year 2021 and registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). We have our headquarter in Nagpur city. We are focused on developing and making use of innovative technology to deliver the services.

  • Our Vision
    To Create an Effective Ecosystem for Financial Empowerment in India.
  • Our Mission
    To Empower People Towards Excellence to Generate Employment.
  • Our Values

    KNOWLEDGE ORIENTED,

    PEOPLE ORIENTED

The company is managed by experienced and energetic professionals with a strong commitment to make this vision A REALITY.
