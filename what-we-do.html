Social Financial Services

Sankalp Micro Association, provides financial services for economically weak, poor especially to women and support with ethics and values as we say "Trust is our only Capital". We not only support them by financially but also also provide end-to-end support to empower them.

At Sankalp Micro Association, we are creating an effective ecosystem for financial empowerment in India.

We prvide various financial services like...

  1. Loans
  2. Deposits
  3. Insurances

Products

Various products available at Sankalp Micro Association are as below...

Savings Account (SA)

Sankalp provides you a No Frills Account which can be operated at zero balance too. A high interest rate of 4% p.a. with numerous SA benefits.

Current Account (CA)

Sankalp provides you a No Frills Account which can be operated at zero balance too. A high interest rate of 2% p.a. with numerous CA benefits.

Fixed Deposits (FD)

Flexible tenures catering to the various requirements of investors ranging from 12 months, 24 months, 36 months, 48 months and 60 months with interest rates being 8.00% p.a., 8.50% p.a., 9.00% p.a., 10.00% p.a. and 11.00% p.a. respectively.

*Note- 0.50% additional interest to women members & Senior Citizens (Members above 50 years).



Mahila Shashtikaran Loan

The Mahila Shashtikaran Loan its called Group loan Minimum Amount INR 15000/to Maximum INR 50000/- Tenure 15 month Rate of interest 26% reducing helping the female poor people.

Shopkeeper Loan

The Shopkeeper Loan Helping the MSME Group Minimum Amount INR 30000/to Maximum INR 200000/- Tenure 12 month to 36 Month Rate of interest 30% reducing helping the Small Businessman.

Personal Loan

The Personal Loan Helping the Salary Person Minimum Amount INR 30000/- to Maximum INR 200000/- tenure 12 Month to 60 Month Interest rate 18% to 32% reducing.

Krishi Sahyata Loan

The Krishi Sahayata Loan Helping the Farmer people Minimum Amount INR 15000/- to Maximum INR 100000/- Tenure 12 Month Interest Rate 24% Reducing & Foreclosure Charges Zero.



Sankalp-18

Tenure: 18 months.
Maturity: INR 5,725 for every INR 5,000 invested.

Sankalp-36

Tenure: 36 months.
Maturity: 1.35 times of the amount invested.

Sankalp Money Back

In Sankalp Money Back, one has to deposit money every month to get a cumulative return at the end of the Every Year.

RD products are available for tenures ranging from 84 months.
